# The Voice of Memory

Photography is incredible. It freezes a split second of joy, a tear rolling down a cheek, or a burst of laughter. But there is one thing photography cannot capture: **sound**.

When you look back at your wedding day in 20 years, you will want to hear your partner's voice cracking as they read their vows. You will want to hear your father's speech, the music that played during your first dance, and the ambient sounds of laughter from your friends.

## Motion Brings Emotion

A cinematic wedding film isn't just a recording; it's a carefully crafted story. At **Nikah Photography**, we don't just set up a tripod and press record. We look for the narrative.

*   **The Anticipation:** The nervous energy in the bride's room.
*   **The Arrival:** The grandeur of the venue in Chittagong.
*   **The Vows:** The core promise that binds two souls.

> "Video captures the movement of the dress, the wind in the hair, and the way he looked at you when you weren't looking."

## Why Invest in Film?

Many couples consider video an "optional extra" to save budget. However, consistently, the #1 regret of couples we speak to who didn't book a cinematographer is exactly that. Photos hang on walls, but films transport you back in time.
